About SeaSell Batumi

Welcome to SeaSell Batumi, the premier marketplace designed specifically for the vibrant and diverse community of Batumi, Georgia.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between locals and expats by providing a seamless, trilingual platform (English, Ukrainian, and Georgian) for buying, selling, and discovering everything you need in the city. Whether you're looking for an apartment in New Boulevard, selling electronics in Old Batumi, or offering professional services, SeaSell makes it easy and secure.

Why Choose Us?

  • Local Focus: Built exclusively for Batumi, featuring specific districts and local currencies (GEL/USD).
  • Multilingual: Breaking down language barriers to connect buyers and sellers from all backgrounds.
  • Direct Communication: Integrated WhatsApp and Telegram buttons for instant, hassle-free contact.
  • Modern & Secure: A fast, mobile-friendly interface powered by robust security.
